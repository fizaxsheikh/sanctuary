Why Sanctuary?

🌍 Community First, Always

This isn’t just an app. It’s a network of people who have each other’s backs. Share warnings. Drop a location pin. Walk each other home, virtually or in real life.

🌈 Inclusive by Design

We see you. Women of color, LGBTQ+ folks, non-binary individuals. Safety isn’t one-size-fits-all, and neither is our approach.

🕵️ Privacy is Power

Safety means staying invisible. From discreet SOS signals to anonymous reporting, your business is yours.

⚡ Tech that Fights Back

Geo-fencing alerts before you enter a high-risk area, AI-powered incident tracking. We predict threats before they happen.

💡 Knowledge is Protection

Self-defense guides, legal resources, mental health support—because being safe means being prepared.

🚀 Real Change, Not Just Talk

We’re collaborating with universities, advocacy groups, and lawmakers to demand real, systemic change.
